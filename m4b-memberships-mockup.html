Questions, answered.

Five questions that always come up before "apply".

Who is eligible?
Professionals actively pursuing their first or next board seat, with 7+ years of leadership experience. Selection is intentional — cohorts are small on purpose.
How does selection work?
Applications are reviewed by the Management Board within five working days. Short interview, short waitlist, small cohort — deliberately.
Where does it meet?
Europe-wide. In-person events in Zurich, London, Frankfurt, Singapore. Monthly masterclasses are virtual. Annual summit rotates cities.
Is it refundable?
Cancel anytime in the first 30 days for a full refund. After that, annual memberships are non-refundable but fully transferable within your cohort.
How does corporate invoicing work?
Annual invoice, Swiss VAT where applicable. Corporate memberships are priced per scope.
