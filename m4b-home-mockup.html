The case for a new kind of director

The risks have changed. The room hasn't.

Climate, cyber, AI governance, workforce composition — the questions a board is now expected to answer are not the questions most boards were assembled to answer.

Millennials4Boards exists to close that gap, deliberately and with the people already sitting in the seat.

We don't want to disrupt the boardroom. We want to finish building it.
